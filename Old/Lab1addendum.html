Besides the included lab, just a couple notes:
  • Make sure you're very careful connecting the voltage regulator (7805). You're less likely to make a mistake if you learn how it works before you solder it in
    • Quick version: The ground pin is shared by both the 9V and the board's supply. The other two pins go only to one or the other
  • You might want to create a +5V and GND rail on each side of the board. You will not need 9V anywhere other than to the 7805, so don't put that on a rail
  • Because we aren't programming them just yet, just ignore any FTDI programmer (though you should probably still add the connections)
  • Basically, learn why you're doing what you're doing before you do what you're doing (simple, right?)
